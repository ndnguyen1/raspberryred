Instrument
ESPN — Perpetual Note Vault

ESPN is a yield primitive that transforms Ethereum's natural volatility into an on-chain yield stream. EthStrategy's Perpetual Note earns yield by selling covered options on Derive, underpinned by long-dated call options on treasury ETH provided by STRAT holders in exchange for leverage exposure.

Capital is split across two strategies: a portion into Lindy, deterministically liquid strategies to cover redemptions, and the remainder into the STRAT convertible note. The vault is designed to wind down to zero with no bad debt.

Liquidity Profile
Stochastic Liquidity
Not a fixed lock

ESPN is stochastically liquid — available liquidity depends on the strike prices of active short calls on Derive. Capital is locked for the duration of each option cycle, creating variable liquidity windows rather than fixed lock periods.

Analogous to a lending market: as liquidity decreases, yield increases — attracting capital and naturally rebalancing utilisation.
