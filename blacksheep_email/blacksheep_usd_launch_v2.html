USD support is now live on Blacksheep.

 

USD Balances

You can now hold USD directly in your Blacksheep account. Deposit, hold a balance, and convert when you're ready.

 

USD ↔ Stablecoin

Swap directly between USD and USDC or USDT. If you invoice in USD but settle on-chain, this removes an entire conversion step.

 

USD ↔ AUD

Move between USD and AUD inside Blacksheep. No need to route through a separate FX provider.
